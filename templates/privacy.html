Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We care about your privacy and data protection. This policy outlines how we handle data processed through our application.

1. Data Collection & Processing

We collect essential account details like email addresses for membership verification. Uploaded DXF files are processed immediately in-memory and are not stored permanently on our servers.

2. Payment Security

We do not store or collect financial data. Payment processing is completely offloaded to Paddle, which manages personal transaction data according to industry security standards.
